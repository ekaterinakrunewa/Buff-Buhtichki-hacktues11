Welcome to Timelink - Where Time is Your Currency!

What is Timelink?

Timelink is a community-driven platform where you exchange time instead of money for everyday tasks and services. Whether you’re offering tutoring, grocery shopping, or small repairs, every hour you contribute earns you Time Credits. You can then spend these credits to receive help from others or redeem them for discounts on health, sports, and financial services.

How to Get Started?

  1. Sign up & create a profile – List the skills or services you can offer.
  2. Earn Time Credits – Help others and track your balance.
  3. Spend or Redeem – Request services or unlock exclusive discounts.

Core Features:

  • Easy registration & login
  • Track your Time Credit balance
  • Offer & request services seamlessly
  • Find the right match through our smart search system

Why Use Timelink?

  • Save money by exchanging skills instead of spending cash.
  • Build a stronger community by helping others.
  • Enjoy exclusive perks through business-sponsored discounts.
